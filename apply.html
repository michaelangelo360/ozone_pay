Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need a valid ID (National ID, Passport, Driver's License, or Voter's ID), proof of income (pay stubs, bank statements, or business records), and a mobile money account for repayments.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are processed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive notification via SMS and email once your application is approved.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through mobile money (75% of customers), bank transfers, and through our network of payment agents.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand that circumstances can change. Contact our support team immediately, and we'll work with you to arrange a revised payment plan.

How do carbon credits work?

When you use our green products, you reduce carbon emissions. These reductions can be quantified as carbon credits, which are applied to reduce your repayment amount, helping you save money while helping the environment.
